# Third-Party Data & Licenses

This project uses the following third-party data sources. Their licenses are reproduced below in compliance with each project's terms.

---

## CORE Conference Portal

Conference ranks (A\*/A/B/C) are sourced from the [CORE Conference Portal](https://portal.core.edu.au/conf-ranks/) — specifically the ICORE2023 ranking list published by the Computing Research and Education Association of Australasia (CORE). The rankings are publicly available for research and educational reference.

---

## CSRankings

The CSRankings indicator (`is_csrankings` field) reflects conferences tracked by [CSRankings](https://csrankings.org) by Emery Berger, published at [github.com/emeryberger/CSrankings](https://github.com/emeryberger/CSrankings) under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike license.

---

## WikiCFP

Fallback deadline data for long-tail conferences is retrieved from [WikiCFP](http://www.wikicfp.com). Usage is limited to public Call-for-Paper metadata for research and educational reference.

---
